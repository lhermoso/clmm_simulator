2/13

The Problem: Impermanent Loss is a Tax on Liquidity

  • LPs routinely underperform HODL: fees often fail to offset IL, especially in volatile pairs.
  • Managing IL today means bespoke bots, spreadsheets, and constant monitoring — not scalable for serious capital.
  • DEXes want deeper, stickier liquidity but can’t promise predictable outcomes to LPs because IL erodes returns.
If DeFi is the future of markets, IL is the hidden tax holding it back.
3/13

Solution: Protocol-Level IL Mitigation

  • On-chain engine that continuously estimates IL and hedges LP exposure using perps/spot to keep portfolios in band.
  • Configurable policies for DEXes and LP managers: different risk bands, coverage levels, and fee splits.
  • Delivered as smart contracts + SDK that any LP vault, DEX, or strategy protocol can plug into.
Today: ≈66% of IL mitigated vs unprotected SOL/USDC LP over the last 60 days with real capital. Roadmap: iterating toward 100% IL mitigation while preserving capital efficiency.
